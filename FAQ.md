**Q: What is Polymarket Bot?**

**A:** Polymarket Bot is an advanced automated trading tool designed to monitor, analyze, and execute trades on **Polymarket** — the largest decentralized prediction market platform. It supports real-time market scanning, smart strategies, and automated order placement.

**Q: Is this bot official?**

**A:** No. This is a third-party open-source automation tool. It is not affiliated with Polymarket or Polymarket.com.
